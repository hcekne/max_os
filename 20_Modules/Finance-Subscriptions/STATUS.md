## Purpose
Track recurring obligations and reduce waste from unused subscriptions.

## Scope (MVP)
- Subscription inventory list (service, amount, cycle, renewal date).
- Utility payment tracking (power, gas, telecom, internet).
- Monthly review checklist for cancellations, downgrades, and anomalies.
