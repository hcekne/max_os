# Workflow - Write an Article

## Goal
Publish a high-quality article from idea to final publish.

## Inputs
- topic or brief
- target audience
- desired outcome (teach, persuade, announce)

## Steps
1. Define one-sentence article promise.
2. Draft outline (hook, 3-5 key points, close).
3. Write first draft quickly.
4. Run one edit pass for clarity and structure.
5. Run one edit pass for tone and examples.
6. Add title options (3-5) and pick one.
7. Publish and save final link.

## Outputs
- final article draft or published URL
- short summary for reuse in social/video

## Quality checklist
- [ ] Clear audience and promise
- [ ] Strong opening and clear structure
- [ ] Concrete examples included
- [ ] Call to action or clear close

## Automation opportunities
- Ask AI to create 3 outline options from the topic.
- Ask AI to suggest title options and improve clarity.
