Repeatable step-by-step processes. Mix of human-led and agent-assisted workflows.

- Each workflow has: Goal, Inputs, Steps, Outputs, Quality Checklist.
- Agent-executable workflows are also listed in `SKILLS.md` at the vault root.
- For purely agent-executable capabilities, see also `15_Skills/`.
- Filename pattern: `Workflow - Name.md`.
- When creating a new workflow, use the template from `99_Templates/TPL - Workflow.md`.
