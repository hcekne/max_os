# Workflow - Content Waterfall from Pillar Article

## Goal
Turn one pillar article into multiple platform-ready assets quickly and consistently.

## Inputs
- final (or near-final) pillar article
- target channels (LinkedIn, X, video, reels, newsletter, etc.)
- audience segments

## Steps
1. Create a repurposing brief from the pillar article:
   - core message
   - key claims
   - proof/examples
   - calls to action
2. Define asset list for this cycle (example):
   - 3-5 LinkedIn posts
   - 1 talking-head video outline
   - 3 short reel scripts
   - 5 X posts
3. Create one note per asset using `TPL - Content Derivative Asset`.
4. Ask AI to generate first drafts per channel with platform constraints.
5. Run human edit pass for voice and factual precision.
6. Finalize publishing schedule and owner.
7. Publish and log performance notes back to each asset note.

## Asset quality rules
- Keep message consistent with source article.
- Adapt tone/length to channel instead of copy-pasting.
- Include one clear CTA per asset.

## Outputs
- packaged derivative assets linked to one pillar article
- channel-ready copy/scripts
- post-publication learnings for next cycle

## Automation opportunities
- AI auto-generation of channel variants from one source brief
- AI conversion of article sections into reel hooks and short scripts
- AI extraction of quote cards / key lines / tweet threads
