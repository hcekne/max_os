# Goals

Use this folder for medium-term and long-term work goals.

## Goals vs Planning
- Goals = direction and outcomes you want to reach.
- Planning = what you will do this week/month/quarter to move toward those goals.

## What to keep here
- one note per major goal
- why it matters
- success criteria
- current status
- links to related projects, content, and plans

## Naming
Use names like:
- `Goal - Become top AI strategy advisor.md`
- `Goal - Build repeatable client delivery engine.md`
