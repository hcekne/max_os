One note per person. Filename: `First Last.md`.

- When creating a new person note, use the template from `99_Templates/TPL - Person.md`.
- Always include: name, organization, role, how we met, last interaction date.
- Cross-link to organizations in `02_Organizations/` and clients in `03_Clients/`.
- Update `last_interaction_date` when processing a new interaction involving this person.
- Do not invent facts about people. Only record what is explicitly stated or observed.
