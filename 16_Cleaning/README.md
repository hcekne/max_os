# Cleaning

Central holding area for historical material and short-retention deletion candidates.

## Structure
- `Archive/` keeps material with historical or retrieval value.
- `Rubbish Bin/` keeps clearly stale or superseded material that should be easy to scan and purge.

## Rules
- Mirror the source path beneath `Archive/` or `Rubbish Bin/`.
- Keep active folders focused on current work only.
- Do not create new distributed archive folders in active project, note, content, or inbox surfaces unless there is a specific local reason.
- Files in `Rubbish Bin/` should usually carry a `delete_after` date.
- Do not put final deliverables, contracts, invoices, legal/commercial documents, or client-provided source material in `Rubbish Bin/`.
